Collection of event tickets

Every month in Pokemon Go, there is a three hour window event on a fixed date, usually on a Saturday or Sunday. As this mobile game is first and foremost a community outdoor game, I took this opportunity to organise events in the city I lived in. The goal was simple : gather people so they could meet and play together, with some lucky people getting a bit of goodies.

I got myself a nice team to help me out, some generous donators, and even sponsorship from a fast-food and an atelier. An association was in project, but I moved away before it could concretize. As part of the events, I designed and drawed little tickets, which people soon began to collect from one month to another. Today, the initiative is still on going without me, and I'm pretty proud of what it has become.
