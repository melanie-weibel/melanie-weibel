Digital drawings

Every since I started drawing, I've wanted to give a try to digital art. Unfortunatly, I simply hated that I couldn't see what I was drawing under my hand, and the ones with screens were waaaaaaaaaaaaaaay out of my budget. So, I decided to wait until the time they would be cheaper, and I would have more money to buy one of it.

My baby. Yes, I cheated a bit, this is not a digital drawing. But what you see here is the accomplishment of a ten years dream, and this is more beautiful than any digital art. (Or not)
